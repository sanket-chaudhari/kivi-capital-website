About us
Who we are
Kivi Capital is an investment management firm located in Gurgaon, India, that specializes in developing quantitative trading strategies and their execution through an automated trading infrastructure.
What we do
We use quantitative analysis and statistical modeling to identify trends and inefficiencies in capital markets, and explore new trading opportunities in liquid exchange traded instruments. By deploying a range of fund management strategies across asset classes, we are able to pursue opportunities that offer the highest potential for capital appreciation.
culture
